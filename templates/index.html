Crop & Carry – Marketplace Platform

Developed Crop & Carry as a full-stack web application during my DBMS coursework, aimed at directly connecting farmers and consumers while eliminating intermediaries and promoting organic product sales. The platform allows farmers to list and manage their agricultural products, while consumers can browse and purchase items based on their needs. Once an order is placed, it is made available to delivery partners, who can accept or reject delivery requests. Upon acceptance, the order is assigned to the respective delivery partner, with a dedicated dashboard to track and update delivery progress, including pickup and final delivery status. After successful delivery, consumers receive email and dashboard notifications to rate both the product and delivery service. The system also provides performance insights by displaying average ratings for farmers and delivery partners on their respective dashboards, ensuring transparency and trust across the platform.
